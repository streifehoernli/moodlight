MoodLight  HS2020
ZHAW ETP Project
Moodlight

is the subject of the Electonics-Project (ETP) starting with fall term 2020 in the third year of the electronics engineering part-time curriculum at the School of Engineering of the Zurich University of Applied Sciences.

Colour space approximated with power LEDs (white, amber, red, green and blue)

LEDs driven by HW (step-down converter) and microcontroller SW (written in C)

User interface on microcontroller evalboard: pushbuttons to chose colour and mode, touchslider to change value and touchgecko to restart (or stop)

For solution specific description see powerLEDs.c

Remote control over serial interface with a protocol based on command strings

User interface as smartphone app (written in Java) which uses the serial protocol over Bluetooth

Colours may be: white, amber, red, green and blue
Colour space preferably: hue, saturation, lightness
Modes could be: midday, sunset moodlight, submarine, doze, awake, party, yoga, ...

Board: Starter Kit EFM32-G8XX-STK Device: EFM32G890F128 (Gecko)

Coding guidelines The official coding guidelines of GNOME were followed. For detailed information, visit: GNOME Coding Guidelines
